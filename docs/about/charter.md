## Vision Statement

Manush-E envisions a future where comprehensive interventions in physical, nutritional, and mental health ensure every child's well-being. We want to prioritize and optimize children's well-being through data-driven decision-making, maximize resource allocation, and provide early intervention, which will ultimately increase children's productivity and quality of life.

## Mission Statement

Our objective is to enable communities and caregivers to ensure every child's health by using data-driven insights to plan and provide comprehensive physical, nutritional, and mental health interventions. We are committed to optimizing resource allocation and promoting early intervention measures that lead to increased productivity, higher quality of life, and a brighter future for all children.

## Community Statement

At Manush-E, our strength lies in our community based approach. We are committed to:

- **Inclusivity and Diversity:** Welcoming contributions and ideas from individuals of all backgrounds.

- **Transparency:** Ensuring open dialogue and clear communication in all project decisions.

- **Collaboration:** Creating an environment where ideas are freely exchanged and evolved through collective effort.

- **Respect and Support:** Upholding a safe, respectful, and encouraging space for everyone and every culture.

- **Privacy:** Respecting and protecting the privacy of all users, especially the children who are our primary users

We believe that every member has a voice, and we encourage active participation through open-source contributions, community discussions, and collaborative projects.
